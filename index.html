Publications

Pneumonia Identification in Chest X-Ray Images using EMD

Pneumonia is a common lung infection where an individual’s alveoli fills up with fluid which forms cloudy like structures. The accurate extent of the disease in infected person is infected is still a challenge for doctors. In this paper, the use of EMD to correctly identify infected pneumonia lungs from normal non-infected lungs is shown. EMD, also known as Earth Mover’s Distance is the distance of two probability distributions over some region D. We preprocessed the images, re-scaling, rotation and normalization of intensity so that we will have a set of uniform size/shape of lungs X-Rays and then we calculated EMD and compared the results.

Video Analytics Based Identification and Tracking in Smart Spaces

Nowadays, video cameras have become a necessary part of everyday life. Video Content Analysis also referred as Intelligent Video Analytics is used to detect, track and analyze the movement and behaviors of objects and humans. The technical uses of video content analytics are face recognition, license plate recognition, tracking a moving object, searching for an object, identifying the geographic location of something, detecting suspicious activities etc. This paper shows and reviews various algorithms and methods for the applications of the video content analysis.
